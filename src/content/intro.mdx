# Introduction to Harems

Harems is a collection of 10,000 Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs); each NFT is a unique digital 
collectible of  Harem Girls covering 235 countries around the world that exist on the Ethereum 
blockchain.  Most of these will be animated - some of these will be live girls.2% of all 10k Harems 
resales will go to the community to be used in whatever form the community wishes. Owners of a Harem 
(minimum of 5 girls) will be spawned one of 11 benefactors i.e. Sultan, Movie Star, Mormon etc.  Each 
benefactor earns an income in Harems tokens; the income of the benefactor is dependent on the quality 
of the benefactor.  And the quality of the benefactor is determined by the value of the girls and other 
material possessions in their Harem.  Harems tokens can be used to purchase other Harems NFTs that would 
increase the value of your Harem i.e. houses, yachts, planes etc. Your benefactor can be upgraded as the 
value of the Harem increases.  And the better your benefactor - the more they earn in Harems tokens.
