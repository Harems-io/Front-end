# Fair Distribution Model

Each Harem Girl costs 0.10 ETH.  There are no price tiers.  Outside of the first
41 Harem Girls – the remaining NFTs will be sold and distributed via a random
airdrop among the buyers on Open Sea.

<br />

# 235 Countries Represented

Every country will have a unique Harem Girl designed by the community of OG
Gentleman’s Club members. The amount of pictures represented by each country
are determined by random algorithms. Each country’s Harem Girl will be represented
based on geographic standards; Brazilian girls are going to look Brazilian and
Kenyan girls will look Kenyan. Depending on the country - there could be unique
characteristics in a picture that relate to a country’s history or background.
Gentleman love knowledge as well as beautiful women.
